The film explores the moral dilemmas and challenges faced by Oppenheimer, along with the profound impact of his work on global politics, warfare, and humanity's understanding of destructive power. With Nolan's signature storytelling and a star-studded cast, "Oppenheimer" promises a deep, introspective look into the psyche of a man whose contributions changed the course of history.
